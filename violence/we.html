    * A secular, Mumbai - based non - profit organisation, We Care believes that investing in women's health
    is essential to building viable urban communities.

    * The We Care team is a dynamic group of 350+ individuals.

    * At the helm, leading this committed team, is a group of men and women
    with decades of experience who are dedicated to our work.

    * We Care targets large public health areas mainly Prevention of Violence against Women and Children.

    Vision
      Healthy Women and Children for a Healthy Urban World

    Our Mission
      We work in partnership with communities and health systems building effective and replicable solutions, empowering women and their families in urban slums to improve their health.
